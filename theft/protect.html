How to Protect Yourself from Identity Theft

  • Shred All Identifying Documents, Bank Slips, etc. When They Are of No More Use to You
  • Shop Online Only on Secure Web Sites
  • Don't Fall for Phishing Scams
  • Beware of Telephone Scams
  • Keep Your Anti-Virus and Anti-Spyware Software Up to Date
  • Check Your Credit Report Often
  • Find Out About Identity Theft Prevention Procedures
  • Secure Your Important Documents
  • Be Vigilant About Your Social Security Number
  • Be Careful With Your Mail (Electronic or Otherwise)
