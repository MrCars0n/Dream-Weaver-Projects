Spr Widgets

What are Spry Widgets?

Spry Widgets are code scripts that provide various forms of interaction on a Web page.

How can Spry Validation improve a form?

Using Spry Validation, you can require that certain fields be completed. You can also confirm whether or not the information provided is in the correct format, such as an e-mail address.

How do Spry Collapsible Panels work?

After inserting a Spry Collapsible Panel, you provide a title for the panel tab and content within panel, you can then show or hide the panel content by clicking on the panel tab.
